Thermography Inspection

Many catastrophic failures and electrical fires start with silent overheating of terminals, busbars, or loose circuit breakers.

Using infrared thermal imaging analysis with calibrated equipment, I precisely locate hidden hot spots before they cause a forced shutdown or severe damage to your installation.
